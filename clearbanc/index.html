Frequently Asked Questions

What does my valuation mean?

Your Clearbanc valuation is a numerical representation of what Clearbanc thinks your business is worth at a point in time. Valuation uses AI and leverages an extensive data infrastructure to analyze a companys position in the market, revenue trajectory, and potential for growth alongside competitors in the vertical.

How much does the Clearbanc Valuation cost?

Your Clearbanc Valuation and weekly insights are free while we are in our public beta.

How is the valuation calculated?

Clearbanc uses a combination of public and private data about your business and feeds that into our proprietary valuation engine. A valuation for each business is generated upon request and our valuation is updated on a weekly basis.

What can I do with my valuation?

Clearbancs Valuation is a representation of what we believe your business is worth at a point in time. We are constantly building new tools to help you take advantage of your valuation, but at the moment you can use your valuation to:
  - See how your business is performing week over week compared to similar companies
  - Meet Buyers interested in buying your business
  - Meet Investors interested in buying a portion of your business
  - See which Clearbanc Beta Products you qualify for
  - See how much Growth Capital you qualify for

Who can see my valuation?

Only you. You can choose to share your valuation with whomever you want but Clearbanc will never share it on your behalf.

Is this an official 409A valuation?

No, Clearbancs valuation is not an official 409A valuation, however, we can introduce you to companies that can provide you with one if you want.

How accurate is the valuation?

There are a variety of reasons the valuation may not be accurate. Valuations fluctuate with market conditions and the accuracy of the data we have available. While we're in beta, we may not have the full picture of the data for your business.

I have questions about my valuation, who can I speak to?

Send an email to valuations@clearbanc.com and someone will get back to you soon.
